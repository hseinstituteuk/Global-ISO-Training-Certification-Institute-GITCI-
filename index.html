About GCICB

The Global CPD & ISO Certification Board (GCICB) is a leading accreditation body recognized worldwide for its commitment to quality, integrity, and excellence in professional development and certification.

We provide CPD approval and ISO accreditation to training providers and courses, ensuring that learners receive the highest standard of education and certification that is globally recognized and respected.

Our mission is to empower professionals across industries by validating their skills and knowledge through trusted certification processes.
